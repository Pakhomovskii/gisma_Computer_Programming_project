
Encourage Remote Work and Virtual Meetings: Leverage technology to minimize the need for travel, in line with the EU's digital single market strategy.
Promote Sustainable Transport: Utilize the EU's initiatives for sustainable transport, including trains and electric vehicles, for necessary business travel.
Participate in EU Emissions Trading System (ETS): For industries covered under the ETS, consider carbon offsetting within the system to compensate for travel-related emissions.
Adopt EU’s Green Public Procurement (GPP) for Travel Services: When procuring travel services, prioritize providers with strong environmental performance.
