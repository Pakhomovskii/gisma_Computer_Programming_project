
Adopt Energy-Efficient Technologies: Follow the EU’s Energy Efficiency Directive by upgrading to energy-efficient appliances and systems.
Implement Renewable Energy Solutions: Align with the EU Renewable Energy Directive by incorporating renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, or biomass, into your energy mix.
Conduct Energy Audits: Regular energy audits are encouraged under the EU Energy Efficiency Directive to identify savings opportunities.
Energy Performance of Buildings: Comply with the Directive on the energy performance of buildings by ensuring that buildings meet energy efficiency standards.

Resources:

The European Commission’s website on Energy Efficiency provides guidelines and best practices.
The EU’s Renewable Energy Directive offers a framework for the promotion of energy from renewable sources.
