
Waste Hierarchy Compliance: Follow the EU Waste Framework Directive, prioritizing waste prevention, followed by reuse, recycling, and recovery, with disposal as the last option.
Circular Economy Action Plan: Implement strategies aligned with the EU’s Circular Economy Action Plan, focusing on sustainable product design, reducing waste, and promoting recycling and reuse.
Eco-Design and Green Public Procurement (GPP): Utilize eco-design principles and GPP for products and services to reduce waste and encourage recycling.
Reporting and Compliance: Ensure compliance with EU waste management reporting requirements to monitor and reduce waste generation.

Resources:

The European Environment Agency (EEA) offers resources and data on waste management and the circular economy.
